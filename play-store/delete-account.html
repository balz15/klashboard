Delete your KlashBoard account

Last updated: June 2026

KlashBoard lets you delete your account yourself. You do not need to email support for a standard deletion request.

How to delete your account

  1. Sign in at https://www.klashboard.com/auth or in the KlashBoard Android app.
  2. Open your profile menu (top-right avatar) and choose Delete account, or go to https://www.klashboard.com/delete-account.
  3. Read what will be removed, check the confirmation box, type DELETE, and confirm.
  4. You will be signed out immediately. Deletion is permanent.

What we delete

  • Your account email, profile name, and login credentials
  • Challenges you created, including members, check-ins, streaks, and chat in those challenges
  • Your participation, daily logs, chat messages, and reactions in challenges you joined
  • Custom challenge icons you uploaded
  • Template submissions tied to your account

What may remain

  • On your device: optional reminder settings in local storage until cleared
  • Backups / logs: short-lived server backups or security logs under our host’s retention policies
  • Other members’ challenges: challenges you did not create remain with their creators; only your data is removed
