KlashBoard

=== SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars) ===
Group habit challenges with daily check-ins, streaks, and accountability.

=== FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars) ===
Turn personal goals into shared challenges with friends.

KlashBoard helps small groups (2–5 people) stay accountable on daily habits — fitness, learning, mindfulness, or anything you track together. Log progress in seconds, see who showed up, and keep momentum when motivation fades.

WHY KLASHBOARD?
• Real accountability — your group sees daily progress
• Fast check-ins — log in under 30 seconds
• Flexible challenges — custom metrics, dates, and templates
• Streaks & leaderboards — friendly competition that keeps you going
• Group chat — stay connected inside each challenge
• Smart reminders — optional daily alerts so you never forget to log

HOW IT WORKS
1. Create or join a challenge with friends
2. Check in each day with simple yes/no or number metrics
3. Track streaks, rankings, and group progress on the dashboard
4. Get nudges when someone slips — or celebrate when everyone hits their goals

PERFECT FOR
• Workout or step challenges with friends
• Reading, meditation, or learning streaks
• Quitting bad habits together
• Any goal that works better with people watching
